About Us

We're Green Guy blue Guy. An independent game studio creating retro-inspired games with modern gameplay.

Every game starts with ideas, feedback, and imagination from my family. Green Guy Blue guy is even named after my two sons (their favorite colors respectively). While development is handled solo, many of our best characters, mechanics, and world-building ideas come from family brainstorming sessions.

We love the games from the 90’s, but believe nostalgia works best when paired with polished mechanics and modern design.

We focus on creating fun action, memorable worlds, and rewarding exploration that keep players engaged from beginning to end.

Built with passion - as an independent studio, we're free to focus on making games that we’d genuinely want to play and we hope you'll enjoy them too!
