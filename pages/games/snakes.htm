Snakes with Fists

An indie retro beat ’em up on Steam

Looking for a retro beat ’em up with modern upgrades? Classic arcade brawler energy - combo combat, pixel art, and yes, the snakes throw hands.

Beat ’em up combat & game feel

  • Combo-first combat - chain hits, read the enemy, keep the pressure on
  • Upgrades that matter - power up your fighters and feel the difference in a fight
  • Classic brawler feel - city alleys, tough fights, big punches
  • Two snakes, two styles - Cobruh hits hard; Conduh moves quick
  • Wander off the path - secrets, side quests, and stuff worth punching

Why this retro beat ’em up?

Snakes with Fists is an indie retro beat ’em up with modern RPG-style progression - built for fans of classic arcade brawlers who want the rush and a reason to poke around every alley. Wishlist the game on Steam.

  • Brawl through packs of goons and keep combos rolling
  • Power up Cobruh and Conduh between scraps
  • Peel off the main path for secrets, side content, and fists

The setting (short version)

Welcome to Morbat City: clean on the outside, sludge underneath. Nexis Corp shines from the billboards. The Sludge Syndicate owns the shadows. You’re two snakes with fists. Diplomacy was never an option.

Dig deeper if you want the conspiracy. Or just keep punching. Both are valid.
