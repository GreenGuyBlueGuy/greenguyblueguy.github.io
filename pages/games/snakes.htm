The Game Play

Snakes with Fists is an adventure RPG with a beat 'em up battle system! This game combines the open-world exploration elements of an RPG—interesting NPCs, side quests, secrets, and a strong narrative—with the fun of fighting in the beat 'em up style!

The World

Morbat City

Once a shining metropolis, Morbat City now decays beneath the grip of hidden rulers and corporate corruption. Citizens are kept obedient through propaganda and carefully controlled information. Beneath the neon lights, rebellion is beginning to stir.

Nexis Corporation

A powerful technology conglomerate posing as Morbat's greatest benefactor. Behind its polished image, Nexis Corp conducts unethical experiments, develops advanced cybernetics, and manipulates the city through its secretive Project Eclipse division.

The Sludge Syndicate

Morbat's most feared criminal network. The Sludge Syndicate controls the city's dark underbelly, extortion rings, and underground operations. Whether they're independent criminals or pawns of a greater conspiracy remains a dangerous question.
