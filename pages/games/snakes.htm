The Game Play

Snakes with Fists is an adventure RPG with a beat 'em up battle system! This game combines the open-world exploration elements of an RPG—interesting NPCs, side quests, secrets, and a strong narrative—with the fun of fighting in the beat 'em up style!

The Opening Story

On a purple planet, in the city of Morbat, lived Cobruh and Conduh, two brothers raised in a crime family called the Sludge Syndicate. They turned away from the criminal organization and are now in hiding, living a simpler life in the Hinterland Hills outside of town.

Each day, Cobruh works in his shop fixing broken-down vehicles, while Conduh teaches science and technology to the local children. Each evening, they hold a self-defense class, hoping to give citizens a fighting chance against the city’s dangers.

One night, a massive explosion rocked the neighborhood. Amid the chaos and destruction, a symbol of the Sludge Syndicate was spray-painted on one of the walls, leaving no mystery about who was to blame. Realizing that there was no escaping the reach of the Sludge Syndicate, the brothers embarked on a relentless mission to end the crime ring—leading them on a journey filled with dark secrets and new, dangerous adversaries.
