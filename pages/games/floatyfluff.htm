Play it right meow!



What is Floaty Fluff?

A casual one-touch mobile game where players avoid obstacles by tapping the screen.

The Pitch:

Point #1 - People love cats, or they hate them.

Point #2 - Balloons are fun (subjective - but generally that is their intent)

Currently, there are no ‘cats tied to a balloon’ games.

So the market is wide open!

We think you can connect the dots, this idea translates into $$$$

The Team:

Green Guy Blue Guy is a family company - literally, not figuratively. We do not say things like “you are like family when you work here”. We are literally family.

We don’t have a cat. Sorry to say, because I know what you are thinking “hey wow, a cat game, I bet the creators have a ton of cats”. The truth is, we don’t even have ONE cat. We love cats, but some of us are allergic to cats - and how do you figure that out? That’s right you've hugged a cat, or pet a cat then rubbed your eyes, oof. All of that being said, we do think cats are pretty swell.

Game Engine:

We made this in Godot. The original concept was made in Solar 2d, which is a great open source engine for cross-platform development. We moved to godot because Solar 2d was having some issues (crashing) after an operating system update.
