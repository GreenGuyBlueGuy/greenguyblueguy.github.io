Still Moving Forward - A Snakes with Fists Dev Update

By Joe Little • 2025

Game development is rarely linear, especially when working independently. External responsibilities have introduced occasional delays, but development on Snakes with Fists continues consistently.

Over time, the project has steadily evolved from an early playable concept into a more refined and cohesive experience. Combat systems are tighter, mechanics are more interconnected, and overall gameplay flow has improved significantly.

The current focus is preparing the demo for a planned summer release window, with additional time dedicated to playtesting, balancing, and polish. Ensuring a strong first impression remains a top priority.

As release approaches, outreach efforts will expand toward indie-friendly creators and communities that enjoy retro-inspired action games. Opportunities for early access feedback will also be introduced to a small group of players.

Community feedback and collaboration continue to play an important role in shaping the direction of the game, particularly during the final stages of demo preparation.

Development remains steady, with ongoing improvements focused on delivering a polished and engaging experience.
