Boilerplate

Green Guy Blue Guy is an independent game studio making retro-inspired games with modern systems. Named after the founder’s two sons (and their favorite colors), the studio ships solo-developed projects shaped by family brainstorming.

Flagship title Snakes with Fists is a classic beat-’em-up with modern progression - combo-driven combat, character upgrades, open-ish exploration with secrets, and two playable snakes who solve problems with their fists. Also from the studio: mobile arcade game Floaty Fluff and jam experiments like The Last Plant.

One-liner: Retro beat-’em-up. Modern upgrades. Snakes that punch.
